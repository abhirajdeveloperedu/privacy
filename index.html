Privacy Policy for Moonshop

Last updated: November 10, 2024

Important: This privacy policy explains how Moonshop collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and name (via Google Sign-In)
  • Purchase History: Records of products purchased and coins spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and time spent in the app
  • Seller Information: If you create a seller account, we collect shop details and product listings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your user account
  • Service Delivery: Processing transactions, managing coins, and delivering purchased products
  • Advertising: Showing relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve features and user experience
  • Communication: Sending important updates about your account or purchases
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud or unauthorized access

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and analytics
    Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In: For user authentication

4. Advertising

Moonshop displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests.

  • AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect information
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings
  • We earn revenue when you watch rewarded video ads
  • Ad viewing is optional and rewards you with in-app coins

5. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard encryption
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access Control: Firebase security rules restrict unauthorized access
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In provides secure authentication

Note: While we implement security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Purchase History: Retained for record-keeping and customer support
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion

7. Children's Privacy

Moonshop is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

  • We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Google (Firebase, AdMob) for app functionality
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We never share your data with:

  • Third-party marketers
  • Data brokers
  • Unauthorized parties

10. Cookies and Tracking

  • We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising
  • Google AdMob may use cookies to serve personalized ads
  • You can control cookies through your device settings

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using Moonshop, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
